    Our Mission
    Operation neverLeftBehind is a government software firm that focuses on creating solutions that help international travelers alleviate unnecessary stress and anxiety when traveling. Our firm is very young, but we are dedicated to building solutions to help all international travelers feel more confident & comfortable anywhere they choose to go.

    The Purpose of Our App
    Our app is designed to help english-speaking travelers visiting francophone countries. We intend for our users to use this app to help them understand words and short phrases in french. In this future, this app will allow english-speaking users to select 10 other languages to translate english words into.

    How to Properly Use our app:
    First, enter the text (in english) for the word you’d like translated and then press the ‘submit’ button. Then, Voilà! The french translation of the word you entered will be available for you to review. !Important Notice: Please be sure to only enter a maximum of two words into the translator.! are still working on our app to enable the translation of full phrases.

    Is This app Available for other languages?
    At the moment, this app is only for users to translate words from english to french. However, we are working on releasing a premium version of this app for users to translate words and phrases from english into 10 different languages! (Be sure to visit in a few months to access this new, exclusive feature. )
